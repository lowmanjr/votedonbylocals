Charleston resident here & amateur foodie.

This started as a fun social experiment with a small group of friends and is now, hopefully, catching on!

I love good dining, learning about new spots, and debating what the “best” places are—whether it's for a burger, a frozen marg, or an espresso martini. That’s why I wanted to create a community of local food and bev rankings voted on.. by locals!

Why?

1. Robots

The standard review sites can be a helpful proxy, but at times they seem to favor businesses that have optimized for an algorithm.

2. Usefulness

A truly local guide should be useful for visitors, too. The bigger vision is to build a network of these guides. Charleston is just the beginning.

The Golden Rule

To keep this project authentic as it grows, there is just one rule.

To vote, you must be a local.
