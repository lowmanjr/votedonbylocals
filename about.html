Our Manifesto

(Voted on) By Locals.. is more than just a list of restaurants. It's an idea. The idea that the best recommendations come from the people who know a city best: the people who live there.

We started this as a simple social experiment because we were tired of the endless scrolling, the sponsored posts, and the algorithm-optimized lists on mainstream review sites. We wanted a simple, honest answer to the question, "Where should we eat tonight?"

Our Core Principles

1. The Golden Rule: To vote, you must be a local.

This is the foundation of everything. It ensures our rankings are an authentic reflection of the community, not influenced by passing tourists or out-of-towners. This keeps the list genuine and trustworthy.

2. Independent Restaurants Only.

We celebrate the unique character of Charleston's local food scene. This platform exclusively features locally-owned, independent restaurants. You won't find national chains or fast-food establishments here.

3. No Ads, No Algorithms, No Sponsored Posts.

This platform is non-commercial. Rankings are calculated using a simple, transparent point system based directly on your votes. There is no way for a restaurant to pay to improve its ranking. Ever.
