Become an Ambassador

The vision for By Locals.. is bigger than just one city. We want to build a network of authentic guides across the country, each one run by a passionate local who knows their city's food scene inside and out.

If this idea resonates with you, and you find yourself wishing a "By Locals" guide existed for your own town, we'd love to hear from you. We're looking for people to launch and lead their own local rankings.

What makes a great ambassador?

  • You're passionate about your city's independent food and drink scene.
  • You're well-connected in the community (or excited to be).
  • You believe in our core principles of authenticity and transparency.
  • You're organized and can help rally your community to vote.

Sound like you? Reach out.
